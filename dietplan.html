Weekly Diet Plan

Monday

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and nuts
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens
  • Snacks: Apple slices with almond butter
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed vegetables

Tuesday

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt with honey and granola
  • Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap
  • Snacks: Carrot sticks with hummus
  • Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with brown rice and vegetables

Wednesday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, and protein powder
  • Lunch: Quinoa salad with chickpeas and vegetables
  • Snacks: Mixed nuts and seeds
  • Dinner: Grilled shrimp with sweet potato and broccoli

Thursday

  • Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and poached egg
  • Lunch: Lentil soup with a side salad
  • Snacks: Sliced cucumber with tzatziki
  • Dinner: Baked chicken with roasted vegetables

Friday

  • Breakfast: Chia pudding with fresh fruit
  • Lunch: Tuna salad with mixed greens
  • Snacks: Bell pepper slices with guacamole
  • Dinner: Grilled steak with mashed cauliflower and green beans

Saturday

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and tomatoes
  • Lunch: Chicken and vegetable stir-fry
  • Snacks: Greek yogurt with berries
  • Dinner: Baked cod with wild rice and asparagus

Sunday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with mixed fruits and nuts
  • Lunch: Veggie burger with a side salad
  • Snacks: Apple slices with peanut butter
  • Dinner: Grilled chicken with quinoa and roasted vegetables
